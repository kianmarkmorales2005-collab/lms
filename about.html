History & Mission

Established in 1999, Kolehiyo ng Lungsod ng Lipa (KLL) serves as a cornerstone of higher education in Batangas. It was founded to provide accessible, high-quality tertiary education to the deserving youth of Lipa City, fostering academic excellence and social responsibility.

vision

A center of human development commited to the pursuit of wisdom, truth, justice, pride, diginit, local and global competitivenes via quality and free education for all qualified client

mission

Established and maintain an academic environment promoting the pursuit of excellence and the total development of its student as human beings, fear of god, love of country and fellowment

Goals

  • Foster the spiritual, intellectual, social, moral, and creative life of its client via free and quality tertiary education.
  • Provide the client with rich and substantial, relevant and wide range of academic disciplines, expose them to varied curricular and co-curricular experiences with nurture, and enhance their personal dedication to commitments to social, moral, cultural, and economic transformations.
  • Work with the government and the community in the pursuit of achieving national developmental goals
  • Develop deserving and qualified clients with different skill of life existence and prepare them for local and global competitiveness

Academic Programs

KLL offers a diverse range of undergraduate programs designed to meet the demands of the modern global workforce:

Business Administration

Focusing on management, marketing, and entrepreneurship.

Computer Science

Building skills in software development, AI, and systems analysis.

Education

Training the next generation of primary and secondary teachers.

Nursing

Providing rigorous clinical training and healthcare theory.

Arts & Communication

Exploring media, journalism, and creative expression.
